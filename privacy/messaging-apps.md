# Secure Messaging Apps

Choose the right encrypted messaging app for privacy.

## Why Encryption Matters

- Protect private conversations
- Prevent eavesdropping
- Secure metadata
- Government surveillance protection
- Corporate data protection

## Top Secure Messaging Apps

### Signal
**Best Overall Privacy**
- End-to-end encryption
- Open source
- Minimal metadata
- Free
- Voice/video calls
- Disappearing messages

### WhatsApp
**Most Popular**
- End-to-end encryption (Signal protocol)
- Owned by Meta/Facebook
- Metadata collection concerns
- 2 billion users
- Cross-platform

### Telegram
**Feature-Rich**
- Optional encryption (Secret Chats)
- Cloud-based
- Large group support
- Channels and bots
- Not default encrypted

### Threema
**Privacy-First**
- Swiss-based
- No phone number required
- End-to-end encrypted
- One-time purchase
- Anonymous usage

### Wire
**Business Solution**
- End-to-end encryption
- Team collaboration
- Video conferencing
- Open source
- EU-based

## Feature Comparison

| App | E2E Default | Open Source | Metadata | Cost |
|-----|-------------|-------------|----------|------|
| Signal | Yes | Yes | Minimal | Free |
| WhatsApp | Yes | No | Collected | Free |
| Telegram | No | Partial | Some | Free |
| Threema | Yes | Partial | Minimal | Paid |
| Wire | Yes | Yes | Minimal | Free/Paid |

## Security Best Practices

- Enable disappearing messages
- Use screen lock
- Verify safety numbers
- Turn off cloud backup
- Use registration lock
- Enable two-step verification

Remember: The most secure app is the one your contacts also use!
